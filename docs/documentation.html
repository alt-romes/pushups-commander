Workout Bot

Dashboard Documentation

A bridge between your chatroom and your workout app

The Workout Bot is a chat bot.

After joining your group, it'll track the exercise you log onto the chat.


Only one user has to pay for and activate the bot as described in the documentation. Afterwards, any group member can type right away +50 pushups to begin.


All exercise data can be viewed through the group's dashboard.


Group members can create challenges for the group.


Not a one-size-fits-all

The Workout Bot doesn't try to satisfy all needs. In particular, if you want to track in detail all your gym workouts, this is probably not for you.
